##  Professional Knowledge Hubs

- **[Canadian Institute of Actuaries – Climate](https://www.cia-ica.ca/actuaries/practice-areas/climate/)**
  - Dedicated hub with publications, practice guidance, scenario analysis, climate risk resources, webinars, and educational material for actuaries working in climate and sustainability.

- **[Canadian Institute of Actuaries – Enterprise Risk Management](https://www.cia-ica.ca/actuaries/practice-areas/enterprise-risk-management/)**
  - Resource center covering enterprise risk management, governance, capital management, emerging risks, and actuarial practice guidance.
