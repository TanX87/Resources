## Catastrophe Modelling

### Fundamentals

- **[Catastrophe Model Insights (Society of Actuaries)](https://www.soa.org/globalassets/assets/files/resources/research-report/2026/2026-cc229-cat-model-insights.pdf)**
  - Introduction to catastrophe (Cat) models, their components, and applications in actuarial risk assessment.

- **[Insights in Cat Modeling (The Actuary Magazine)](https://www.theactuarymagazine.org/insights-cat-modeling/)**
  - Overview of catastrophe modelling concepts, emerging trends, and actuarial applications.

- **[Catastrophe Models (Institute and Faculty of Actuaries)](https://www.actuaries.org.uk/system/files/field/document/C02%20Cat%20Models.pdf)**
  - Introductory guide explaining catastrophe models, their structure, strengths, limitations, and practical uses.

- **[Catastrophe Modelling in Non-Life Insurance (ASTIN Colloquium)](https://actuaries.org/app/uploads/2025/08/powerdot-ASTIN2014-krvavych.pdf)**

- **[Life & Health Catastrophic Event Risks (Society of Actuaries)](https://www.soa.org/globalassets/assets/files/resources/research-report/2024/lifehealth-catastrophic-event-risks.pdf)**
