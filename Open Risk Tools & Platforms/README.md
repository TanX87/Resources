##  Open Risk Tools & Platforms

- **[CatRiskTools (Insurance Development Forum)](https://www.insdevforum.org/tools-data-sets/catrisktools/)**
  - Open catalogue of catastrophe risk models, datasets, and modelling resources. Designed to help users identify available models by country and peril for disaster risk and insurance applications. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

- **[Risk Data Library (GFDRR / World Bank)](https://docs.riskdatalibrary.org/en/latest/)**
  - Open standard and documentation for organizing, publishing, and sharing disaster and climate risk data, including hazard, exposure, vulnerability, and loss datasets. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}
