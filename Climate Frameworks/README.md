### TCFD
- **Implementing the TCFD Recommendations (Institute and Faculty of Actuaries & IEMA)**
  - Practical guidance for applying the TCFD recommendations in organizations.
  - https://www.actuaries.org.uk/system/files/field/document/J27006_IEMA_TCFD_Guide_V4.pdf
- **SASB–CDSB TCFD Implementation Guide**
  - Explains how sustainability standards support implementation of the TCFD recommendations.
  - https://www.cdsb.net/sites/default/files/sasb_cdsb-tcfd-implementation-guide-a4-size-cdsb.pdf
- **TCFD Good Practice Handbook (CDSB)**
  - Best practices and real-world examples for preparing climate-related financial disclosures.
  - https://www.cdsb.net/sites/default/files/tcfd_good_practice_handbook_v5_pages.pdf
- **TCFD Guidance on Metrics, Targets and Transition Plans**
  - Guidance for selecting, measuring, and disclosing climate-related metrics and targets.
  - https://assets.bbhub.io/company/sites/60/2021/07/2021-Metrics_Targets_Guidance-1.pdf
