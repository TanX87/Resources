## Climate Disclosure & Reporting

### IFRS S1 & S2

- **[Financial Disclosure Standards and Actuarial Considerations (Society of Actuaries)](https://www.soa.org/globalassets/assets/files/resources/research-report/2024/issb-fin-disclosure-standards.pdf)**
  - Explains the role of actuaries in applying the ISSB sustainability disclosure standards.

- **[International Actuarial Note 200 (IAN 200) – Exposure Draft (IAA)](https://actuaries.org/app/uploads/2025/10/IAA_IAN200_ExposureDraft_17Oct2025_Clean.pdf)**
  - Practical actuarial guidance supporting the application of IFRS S1 and IFRS S2.

- **[IFRS S2 Climate-related Disclosures – IAA Webinar Slides](https://actuaries.org/app/uploads/2025/10/IAA_IFRS-S2_Webinar_Slides_Final.pdf)**
  - Presentation explaining IFRS S2 and its actuarial implications.

### TCFD

- **[Implementing the TCFD Recommendations (IFoA & IEMA)](https://www.actuaries.org.uk/system/files/field/document/J27006_IEMA_TCFD_Guide_V4.pdf)**
  - Practical guidance for implementing the TCFD recommendations within organizations.

- **[SASB–CDSB TCFD Implementation Guide](https://www.cdsb.net/sites/default/files/sasb_cdsb-tcfd-implementation-guide-a4-size-cdsb.pdf)**
  - Demonstrates how SASB and CDSB standards support TCFD implementation.

- **[TCFD Good Practice Handbook (CDSB)](https://www.cdsb.net/sites/default/files/tcfd_good_practice_handbook_v5_pages.pdf)**
  - Real-world examples and best practices for climate-related financial disclosures.

- **[TCFD Guidance on Metrics, Targets and Transition Plans](https://assets.bbhub.io/company/sites/60/2021/07/2021-Metrics_Targets_Guidance-1.pdf)**
  - Guidance for selecting, measuring, and disclosing climate-related metrics and targets.
